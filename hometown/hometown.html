PURWOKERTO

Banyumas District

The Satria City

Purwokerto is the capital city of Banyumas District, Central Java, Indonesia. As the district capital, the name Purwokerto is more popular than Banyumas. This is because previously Purwokerto had the status of an administrative city. Satria City was promoted as the nickname for Purwokerto City because the Banyumas area gave birth to many figures or heroes who contributed to Indonesia's independence and development, or who had a warrior spirit. Big figures from Banyumas such as General Gatot Soebroto, Lieutenant General TNI (Posthumous) Soeprapto, and Prof. Dr. Margono Soekarjo. Apart from that, Satria was chosen as an acronym for Sejahtera (Prosperous), Adil (Fair), Tertib (Orderly), Rapi (Neat), Indah (Beautiful) and Aman (Safe). The language used by the people of Purwokerto is the Banyumasan dialect of Javanese or also called Ngapak.

One of the historical monuments in Purwokerto is the Development Monument (which is in the picture). The name of the Development Monument itself is not without reason, this is because this Monument is part of the Universal Planned Development Stages program. This Development Monument was a gift from Ir Sukarno and also Prime Minister Nikita Khrushchev (Russian Prime Minister). The process of laying the first stone was carried out by the first President of the Republic of Indonesia, Ir Soekarno, as written on the inscription "First Ayoenan Cangkoel".
